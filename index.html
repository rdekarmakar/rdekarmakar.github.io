About Me

As a seasoned Data Engineering Lead with over 12 years of hands-on experience, specialized in designing and building scalable, optimized data-intensive applications. My expertise spans a wide array of technologies including Apache Spark, Hadoop, Python (PySpark), Databricks, Kafka, Airflow, and various ETL tools. I have a strong background in cloud services, particularly Azure and AWS, along with proficiency in handling RDBMS systems like Oracle, Teradata, and SQL Server.

In addition to my technical skills, I excel in Data Warehouse (DWH) ETL advanced concepts, data profiling, performance tuning, and optimization. I have built large-scale systems for data ingestion, exploratory data analysis, data munging, and ensuring data quality. My experience extends to data modeling, encompassing both Data Vault and Dimensional model in BI/DW contexts. Furthermore, I am proficient in reporting tools like Tableau and Power BI.

I am passionate about Data Science and Machine Learning, possessing strong math, statistics, and algorithm knowledge. My hands-on experience includes image processing using Python modules and predictive modeling techniques. I am well-versed in the full project lifecycle, from POC to code deployment, and have a solid track record of managing cross-functional teams and delivering successful projects. With a 'can do' attitude and a continuous learning mindset, I am always ready to explore new technologies and implement innovative solutions.

Moreover, I bring solid leadership and team-building skills to the table, having effectively managed priorities across cross-functional teams and geographies. I am known for my resourcefulness, 'can do' attitude, and a proven track record of delivering successful projects. I thrive on challenges, am self-motivated, and always ready to take on new challenges and technologies.
